About Movie Log

I wanted to create this project because I have been into watching movies recently this year and been watching many movies and shows. On many streaming platforms and wanted a way to keep track of what I have watched and what I want to watch. Whether indie or massive popular movie, I have been watching movies with my family and friends, I bought my sister a projector and we watch them on there.

I also wanted to create this project to learn more about PWA or mobile development at the same time. I utilized the Materialize CSS framework and library and demonstrated a layout similar to the course example and added PWA features such as manifest.json and service workers and provided offline functionality.
